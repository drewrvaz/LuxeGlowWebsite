Preparing for your spray tan

  • Exfolation/shave several hours ahead (shoot for min of 4 hrs) of your spray tan appt to avoid discoloration.
  • Avoid moisturizer or other body products on the skin including deodorant.
  • Complete any spa/facial treatments the day prior.
  • Wear dark loose-fitting clothing with open-toed sandals to your appointment.
  • Manicure/pedicure done prior to the spray tan.

Aftercare for your spray tan

  • Do not get wet or sweat until you rinse after your spray tan has fully developed. Wash hands carefully and try to only wash inside of palms until you have taken a shower the following day.
  • Avoid tight fitting clothing that can rub or remove any formula.
  • Do not exfoliate until you are ready for your tan to fade.
  • Regularly moisturize to ensure the spray tan is going to fade evenly.
  • Avoid anything that can strip your tan such as chemicals, chlorine, bandages, etc...
  • Be mindful of crossing your legs, rubbing your eyes, scratching your face, etc...
